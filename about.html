EZPark & Ride

This web application is built to provide real-time parking availability data to the users of Park and Ride facilities. When sensors installed in each Park & Ride lot collects real-time data about the available number of spaces in the lot, the web application dynamically visualizes it and updates the website frequently to provide real-time parking data.

To help users easily recognize the availability situations of the parking lots, I use three colors to group information. The thresholds setting for color-coding of parking availability is based on 686 survey responses during the user research stage.

  • Green Icon

    The parking lot is mostly open. Available Spaces: >50.

  • Gold Icon

    The parking lot is mostly full. Available Spaces: >=20.

  • Red Icon

    The parking lot is full. Available Spaces: < 20.


Also, once the users click each marker, they could find specific information about the Park & Ride they are checking, including its address, zip code, total number of spaces, and real-time available spaces. The design of combining both color coding and count of available stalls is based on 686 survey responses during the user research stage.


In the navigation bar, you can also access to:

  • Popular Times

    It is filtered by day of the week for users to check how popular the Park & Ride facility might be during that day. The prediction data is gained through doing streaming data analysis and machine learning with Power BI.

  • Google Map

    It helps users to directly go to check the route through google map based on the Park and Ride they decides to drive to.

  • King County Transit Center Information

    It helps direct users to this website if they want to check some other relevant transit information regarding certain Park and Rides.
